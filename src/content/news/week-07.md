## Multi-Omics Integration Framework

A new framework enables seamless integration of transcriptomics, proteomics, and epigenomics data with improved cell-type resolution for complex tissue analysis.

## RNA Velocity Estimation Tools Update

New open-source tools simplify RNA velocity computation, reducing memory requirements and enabling analysis on larger single-cell datasets.

## Microbiome Functional Profiling Pipeline

An improved pipeline enhances functional annotation of metagenomic samples, providing better pathway coverage and strain-level resolution.

## Long-Read Genome Assembly Benchmark

A comprehensive benchmark evaluates assembly quality across plant and animal genomes, highlighting best practices for telomere-to-telomere completion.

## Protein Design with Diffusion Models

Recent advances in diffusion-based protein design show improved sampling of novel folds, with potential applications in therapeutics and enzyme engineering.
